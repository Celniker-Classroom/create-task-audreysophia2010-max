Hangman

How to play: Welcome to hangman! The goal of this game is to guess the word before the drawing is complete. The player guesses one letter at a time. Correct letters are written in the blanks; incorrect letters are listed, and a body part is added to the gallows drawing. If the whole word is uncovered, you win! If the hangman gets all 6 body parts, you lose. Good luck!
